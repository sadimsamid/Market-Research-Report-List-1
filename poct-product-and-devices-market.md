<p><p>Point-of-Care Testing (POCT) products comprise two main market types: consumables and devices. Consumables include testing materials like reagents and cartridges that are essential for conducting tests, often used in single or repeated assays. Devices refer to the instruments and equipment, such as blood glucose meters or handheld diagnostic units, that facilitate the testing process. Together, these components enhance rapid diagnostic capabilities, improving patient management and outcomes by enabling immediate results and reducing the need for centralized laboratory testing.</p></p>

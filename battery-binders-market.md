<p><p>The battery binders market is segmented into anode binders and cathode binders, each crucial for enhancing battery performance. Anode binders primarily support the adhesion of active materials in anodes, improving energy density and cycle stability. Meanwhile, cathode binders play a similar role in cathodes, ensuring structural integrity and conductivity. Both types of binders influence battery efficiency, longevity, and overall performance, making them essential components in lithium-ion and other advanced battery technologies, thereby driving market growth in the energy sector.</p></p>

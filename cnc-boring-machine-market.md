<p><p>The CNC boring machine market comprises primarily two types: vertical boring machines and horizontal boring machines. Vertical boring machines are designed for drilling operations where the workpiece is positioned vertically, allowing for greater visibility and accuracy in deep holes and large parts. Horizontal boring machines, on the other hand, orient the workpiece horizontally, providing stability for heavier components and facilitating the machining of larger parts with multiple operations. Both types enhance precision and efficiency in industrial applications.</p></p>

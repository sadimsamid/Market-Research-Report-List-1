<p><p>The dry film thickness measurement market encompasses various techniques, including Eddy Current, Magnetic Induction, Optical, and Ultrasonic methods. Eddy Current uses electromagnetic principles to measure non-ferrous coatings on conductive substrates. Magnetic Induction targets ferrous substrates to gauge thickness through magnetic fields. Optical methods utilize light reflection to determine thickness on non-metallic surfaces. Ultrasonic technology employs sound waves to measure thickness in coatings, offering precision across various materials, thus catering to diverse industrial needs in quality control and compliance.</p></p>

Rotary hydraulic diaphragm pumps employ a rotating mechanism for continuous fluid movement, ideal for low-viscosity applications. Other market types encompass specialized designs and hybrid models that combine features from both reciprocating and rotary systems, catering to unique industrial needs and enhancing operational efficiency in various fluid handling processes.</p></p>

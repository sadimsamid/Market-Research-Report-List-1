<p><strong>The Gas Detector Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Gas Detector Tubes</li><li>Dosimetric Gas Detector Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas detector tube market includes two main types: colorimetric and dosimetric gas detector tubes. Colorimetric tubes change color upon exposure to specific gases, providing immediate visual results that indicate gas concentration levels. They are typically used for spot measurements. Dosimetric tubes, on the other hand, measure gas concentration over a period, utilizing absorption to quantify exposure levels. They are ideal for personal monitoring or assessing long-term exposure risks in various industries, enhancing workplace safety and health compliance.</p></p>

<p><p>The hair removal tools market encompasses various types, primarily epilators, laser, and intense pulsed light (IPL) devices. Laser hair removal uses concentrated light to target and destroy hair follicles, leading to permanent reduction over time. IPL devices emit broad-spectrum light to diminish hair growth by targeting pigment in the hair, providing a versatile at-home option for users seeking effective results.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Ruled Gratings</li><li>Holographic Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diffraction gratings market is categorized mainly into ruled gratings and holographic gratings. Ruled gratings are produced by physically engraving lines on a surface, offering high efficiency and precision for various optical applications. Holographic gratings, on the other hand, utilize laser interference techniques to create a periodic structure, yielding enhanced performance across a broader wavelength range. Both types are essential in spectrometry, telecommunications, and imaging systems, serving diverse industries like defense, healthcare, and research.</p></p>

<p><p>The embedded sensor market encompasses various types, including accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, and combo sensors. Accelerometers measure acceleration and tilt, enabling motion detection in devices. Gyroscopes gauge rotation and orientation, essential for navigation and stability. Magnetometers detect magnetic fields, crucial for compass applications. Combo sensors integrate multiple functions, offering enhanced capabilities in a single unit, thus saving space and cost. Together, these sensors play a pivotal role in applications ranging from smartphones and wearables to automotive and industrial systems.</p></p>

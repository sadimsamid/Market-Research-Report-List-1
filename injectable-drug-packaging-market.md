<p><ul><li>Ampules</li><li>Vials</li><li>Cartridges</li><li>Bottles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injectable drug packaging includes various types designed to ensure medication safety and ease of use. Ampules are sealed glass or plastic containers that protect contents from contamination, ideal for single-use drugs. Vials are small bottles, typically with rubber stoppers, suitable for multi-use or larger volumes. Cartridges are pre-filled devices, often used in pen injectors, offering convenience for patients. Bottles, usually larger and multi-dose, store bulk injectable solutions. Each type is designed to meet specific storage and administration needs.</p></p>

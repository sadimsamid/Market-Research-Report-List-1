<p><p>The Airbag Fabric Market consists of various types, primarily categorized into Flat Airbag Fabric and OPW (Open-Polyester Weave) Airbag Fabric. Flat Airbag Fabric is designed for traditional airbag applications, providing durability and puncture resistance for frontal airbags in vehicles. In contrast, OPW Airbag Fabric features a unique open-weave structure that enhances air permeability, making it suitable for side airbags and reducing deployment time. These specialized fabrics are critical for ensuring the safety and effectiveness of airbag systems in automotive applications.</p></p>

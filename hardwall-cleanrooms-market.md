<p><strong>The Hardwall Cleanrooms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Cleanroom</li><li>Negative Pressure Cleanroom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hardwall cleanroom market comprises two primary types: positive pressure cleanrooms and negative pressure cleanrooms. Positive pressure cleanrooms maintain a higher internal air pressure than the external environment, preventing contamination from outside. They are ideal for activities requiring a sterile environment, such as pharmaceuticals and biotechnology. Conversely, negative pressure cleanrooms operate with lower internal pressure, ensuring that contaminants are contained within, making them suitable for handling hazardous materials in industries like healthcare and chemical production. Both types are crucial for maintaining strict cleanliness standards.</p></p>

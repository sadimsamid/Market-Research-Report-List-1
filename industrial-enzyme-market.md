<p><p>The industrial enzyme market is categorized into several key types, primarily including carbohydrases, proteases, lipases, and others. Carbohydrases break down carbohydrates, widely used in food and beverage production. Proteases catalyze protein degradation, essential in industries like pharmaceuticals and detergents. Lipases facilitate fat breakdown, crucial for food processing and biodiesel production. The "others" category encompasses a variety of enzymes, such as nucleases and cellulases, utilized across diverse applications, from textile manufacturing to biofuels, highlighting the versatility of enzyme technologies.</p></p>

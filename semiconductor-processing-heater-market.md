<p><p>The semiconductor processing heater market is categorized into several types, including immersion heaters, pedestal heaters, platen heaters, and others. Immersion heaters directly heat liquids in a tank, ensuring uniform temperature control. Pedestal heaters provide heating from below, often used in equipment setups for stability. Platen heaters deliver consistent heat through flat surfaces, ideal for wafer processing. Other types include specialized heating solutions tailored for unique applications within semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing efficiency and precision in the fabrication process.</p></p>

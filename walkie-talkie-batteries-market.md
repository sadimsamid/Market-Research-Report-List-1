<p><p>The Walkie Talkie Batteries market consists of three primary types: Ni-Cd, Ni-MH, and Li-ion batteries. Ni-Cd (Nickel-Cadmium) batteries are known for their durability and resistance to extreme temperatures, but suffer from memory effect. Ni-MH (Nickel-Metal Hydride) batteries offer higher capacity and are more environmentally friendly, making them suitable for extended use. Li-ion (Lithium-ion) batteries are lightweight, have a longer lifespan, and provide higher energy density, making them increasingly popular for modern walkie talkies in various applications.</p></p>

Key types include Alumina (Al2O3), known for its high thermal stability and electrical insulation; Aluminum Nitride (AlN), which offers excellent thermal conductivity and low dielectric constant; and Silicon Nitride (Si3N4), valued for its mechanical strength and thermal resistance. Other ceramics may include materials like zirconia or mixed oxides, each providing unique advantages depending on specific application requirements in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>

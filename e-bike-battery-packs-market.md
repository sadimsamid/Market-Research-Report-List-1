<p><p>The e-bike battery pack market primarily features two main types: lead-acid and lithium-ion batteries. Lead-acid batteries are cost-effective, relatively heavy, and have a lower energy density, making them suitable for budget-friendly e-bikes with less performance demand. In contrast, lithium-ion batteries are more popular due to their lighter weight, higher efficiency, and longer lifespan, providing better range and charging times. The choice between these types often depends on factors like cost, weight considerations, and desired performance.</p></p>

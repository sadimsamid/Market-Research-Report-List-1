<p><strong>The API Tanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Roof</li><li>External Floating Roof</li><li>Internal Floating Roof</li><li>Dome External Floating Roof</li></ul></p>
<p><p>API Tanks represent various market types for chemical and liquid storage solutions. Fixed Roof tanks have a permanent, rigid roof offering protection from environmental elements. External Floating Roof tanks feature roofs that float on the liquid surface, reducing vapor emissions. Internal Floating Roof tanks also have a floating mechanism but are enclosed within a fixed structure, minimizing evaporation losses. Dome External Floating Roof tanks combine the benefits of a floating roof with a dome structure for enhanced durability and reduced emissions.</p></p>

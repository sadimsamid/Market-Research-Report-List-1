<p><strong>The Uncooled Infrared Imaging Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near Infrared</li><li>Shortwave Infrared</li><li>Mid-wave Infrared</li><li>Long-wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled infrared imaging equipment market is segmented into four main types based on wavelength ranges. Near Infrared (NIR) captures images in the 750-1400 nm range, often used in environmental monitoring. Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) operates between 1400-3000 nm, ideal for semiconductor and agricultural applications. Mid-wave Infrared (MWIR), spanning 3000-5000 nm, is commonly employed in defense and industrial sectors. Long-wave Infrared (LWIR), ranging from 8000 nm to 14,000 nm, is mainly utilized for thermal imaging in surveillance and building inspections.</p></p>

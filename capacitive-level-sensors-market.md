<p><ul><li>Switch Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive level sensors are classified into two main types: switch type and continuous type. Switch type sensors detect specific level thresholds, triggering an on/off response to control processes or alarms. They are ideal for applications requiring discrete level measurement. In contrast, continuous type sensors provide real-time level measurement by delivering a continuous output signal proportional to the detected level, making them suitable for precise monitoring in various industrial applications. Both types utilize capacitance changes to assess material levels.</p></p>

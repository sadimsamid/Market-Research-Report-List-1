<p><p>The premium wireless routers market is segmented into three types: Single Band, Dual Band, and Tri Band routers. Single Band routers operate on a single frequency, typically 2.4 GHz, suitable for basic internet needs. Dual Band routers support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, offering enhanced speed and reduced interference for multitasking. Tri Band routers include an additional 5 GHz band, allowing for optimal performance in high-demand environments, making them ideal for multiple users and devices requiring stable and high-speed connections.</p></p>

<p><p>The Micro Thermoelectric Coolers (TEC) market is primarily divided into two types: Single Stage and Multistage Micro Thermoelectric Coolers. Single Stage TECs are designed for applications requiring moderate temperature differences, providing efficient cooling in compact spaces. In contrast, Multistage TECs are engineered for applications needing lower temperatures, utilizing multiple stages to achieve greater temperature differentials. Both types are utilized in various sectors, including electronics cooling, medical devices, and automotive applications, contributing to advancements in thermal management technologies.</p></p>

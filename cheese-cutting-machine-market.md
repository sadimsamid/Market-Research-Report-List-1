<p><p>The cheese cutting machine market is divided into two main types: ultrasonic and classical. Ultrasonic cheese cutting machines use high-frequency sound waves to slice cheese with precision, minimizing waste and ensuring a clean cut, which is ideal for soft cheeses. In contrast, classical cheese cutting machines rely on traditional blades or wires, suitable for various cheese types but may cause uneven cuts. Each type has its applications, catering to different production needs and cheese characteristics within the food industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The Sanitary Actuated Butterfly Valve market is categorized into three main types: Manual, Electric, and Pneumatic. </p><p>Manual butterfly valves require human operation for opening and closing, offering simplicity and reliability in low-demand applications. Electric types utilize electric actuators for precise control and remote operation, ideal for automated systems. Pneumatic valves operate using compressed air, providing quick actuation and responsiveness, suitable for high-speed processes. Each type serves specific industry needs, ensuring efficient flow control in sanitary applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The epoxy-based instant adhesive market is divided mainly into cold cure and heat cure adhesives. Cold cure adhesives are designed for rapid bonding at room temperature, suitable for a variety of materials and applications without requiring additional curing mechanisms. In contrast, heat cure adhesives require elevated temperatures to achieve optimal bonding strength and durability, making them ideal for high-performance applications where enhanced thermal and chemical resistance is needed. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs, emphasizing versatility and effectiveness.</p></p>

<p><p>The electric crawler excavator market comprises various types, including backhoe excavators and front shovel excavators. Backhoe excavators are versatile machines featuring a digging arm at the rear and a loading bucket at the front, ideal for tasks such as trenching and material handling. Front shovel excavators, on the other hand, are designed primarily for digging and material excavation, with a large bucket and a boom that allows for efficient digging in deeper applications. Both types leverage electric power for enhanced sustainability and reduced emissions.</p></p>

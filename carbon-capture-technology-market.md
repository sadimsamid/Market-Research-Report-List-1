<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Carbon Capture Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Combustion Carbon Capture</li><li>Oxy-Combustion Carbon Capture</li><li>Post-Combustion Carbon Capture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon capture technology encompasses three main market types: </p><p>1. **Pre-Combustion Carbon Capture** involves removing CO2 from fossil fuels before combustion, effectively producing hydrogen and capturing emissions early in the process.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Oxy-Combustion Carbon Capture** uses pure oxygen for combustion, resulting in a flue gas that primarily contains CO2 and water vapor, simplifying capture.</p><p>3. **Post-Combustion Carbon Capture** captures CO2 from exhaust gases after combustion, commonly applied in existing power plants to reduce greenhouse gas emissions effectively. Each method targets carbon reduction through different stages of the energy production process.</p></p>

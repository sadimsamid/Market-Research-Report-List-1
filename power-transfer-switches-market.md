<p><p>Power Transfer Switches (PTS) are essential for managing power supply during outages. The market is segmented into two main types: automatic and manual switches. Automatic transfer switches (ATS) detect power loss and seamlessly switch to a backup power source without human intervention, ensuring minimal downtime. In contrast, manual transfer switches require users to manually switch between power sources, offering a cost-effective option for situations where immediate switching is not critical. Both types serve distinct needs in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>

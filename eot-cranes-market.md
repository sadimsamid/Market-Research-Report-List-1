<p><p>EOT cranes, or Electric Overhead Traveling cranes, are classified into various types based on their design and application. Single girder cranes are lightweight, suitable for smaller loads, while double girder bridge cranes provide higher lifting capacities and larger spans. Gantry cranes operate on ground-level rails, ideal for outdoor use and heavy loads. Monorails consist of a single rail, facilitating efficient material handling in confined spaces. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, optimizing lifting and transportation processes across various sectors.</p></p>

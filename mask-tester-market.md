<p><p>The mask tester market encompasses three primary types: Fit Testers, Integrity Testers, and Performance Testers. Fit Testers assess the proper fit of respiratory protective equipment, ensuring a secure seal on the wearer’s face. Integrity Testers evaluate the mask's structural completeness and detection of leaks, ensuring adequate protection. Performance Testers analyze the filtration efficiency and airflow characteristics of masks, ensuring they meet safety standards. Together, these testing types are crucial for ensuring that masks provide effective protection in various environments.</p></p>

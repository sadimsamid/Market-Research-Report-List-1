Preconditioned air units provide climate control for aircraft, enhancing passenger comfort. Electrical ground power units supply electricity to planes while parked, eliminating the need for onboard power. Stand entry guidance systems assist pilots in maneuvering aircraft safely to and from their gates, improving efficiency and reducing the risk of ground accidents. Each component enhances airport operations and passenger experience.</p></p>

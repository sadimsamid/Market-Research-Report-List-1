<p><strong>The Mineral Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epoxy Mineral Casting</li><li>Polymer Minreal Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mineral castings market primarily comprises two main types: epoxy mineral casting and polymer mineral casting. Epoxy mineral casting utilizes epoxy resins combined with mineral fillers to create a durable, high-performance material ideal for precision applications, such as machine bases and high-stability components. Polymer mineral casting, on the other hand, incorporates polymers, offering enhanced flexibility and resilience. Both types are valued for their superior mechanical properties, excellent dimensional stability, and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Espresso machines are categorized into three main market types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require users to control the entire brewing process, offering complete hands-on engagement. Semi-automatic machines provide automated water delivery while allowing users to manage grinding and tamping, striking a balance between control and convenience. Fully automatic machines handle the entire process, from grinding to brewing, with minimal user input, catering to those seeking efficiency and consistency in their coffee experience.</p></p>

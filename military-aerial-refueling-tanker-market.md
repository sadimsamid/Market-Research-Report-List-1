<p><ul><li> Hose-Type Aerial Refueling</li><li>Hard-Tube Aerial Refueling </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military aerial refueling tanker market encompasses two primary types: hose-type and hard-tube aerial refueling systems. Hose-type aerial refueling utilizes flexible hoses that deliver fuel to receiving aircraft, allowing for greater maneuverability during the refueling process. In contrast, hard-tube aerial refueling involves a rigid tube connection, providing a more stable and secure transfer of fuel but with reduced aircraft movement capability. Both systems are essential for extending the operational range and endurance of military aircraft in various missions.</p></p>

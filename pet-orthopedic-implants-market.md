<p><p>The pet orthopedic implants market encompasses various types used in veterinary surgery to address musculoskeletal issues. Plates are metal devices that stabilize fractures by holding bone fragments together. Screws provide additional fixation for these plates or directly anchor bones. Pins and wires are less invasive options for securing bone pieces, while staples facilitate the repair of soft tissues. Joint replacement implants mimic natural joints, restoring mobility in pets suffering from severe joint degeneration, ensuring improved quality of life and mobility.</p></p>

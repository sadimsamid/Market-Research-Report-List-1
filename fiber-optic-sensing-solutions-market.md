<p><p>The Fiber Optic Sensing Solutions market is primarily categorized into two types: Distributed Sensing and Fiber Optic Point Sensing. Distributed Sensing utilizes a single optical fiber to provide continuous measurement along its length, making it ideal for applications like temperature and strain monitoring over large areas, such as pipelines or railways. In contrast, Fiber Optic Point Sensing focuses on specific locations for precise measurements, often used in environments where localized data is critical, such as structural health monitoring or environmental sensing.</p></p>

<p><p>The telecommunications cable market comprises several key types: transmission lines, waveguides, and optical fibers. Transmission lines carry electrical signals over distances, essential for traditional communication networks. Waveguides facilitate the transmission of microwave and radio frequency signals, crucial for satellite and radar applications. Optical fibers utilize light to transmit data at high speeds, offering superior bandwidth and minimal signal loss, making them ideal for modern telecommunications infrastructure. Together, these components enable efficient data transfer and connectivity across various platforms.</p></p>

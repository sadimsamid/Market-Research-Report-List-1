<p><p>Natural flocculants are biodegradable substances derived from natural sources that aid in the aggregation of particles in liquids, promoting sedimentation and clarification. These materials are increasingly preferred over synthetic alternatives due to their eco-friendliness and lower health risks. Natural flocculants are derived from various sources, including plants, algae, and minerals, and are widely used in water treatment, wastewater management, and various industrial processes.</p><p>The Natural Flocculant Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.50% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in extraction and formulation technologies are enhancing the effectiveness and application range of natural flocculants.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a growing preference for plant-based flocculants, such as guar gum and starch, due to their efficacy and safety.

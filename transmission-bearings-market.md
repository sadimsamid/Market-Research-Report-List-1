<p><p>Transmission bearings are vital components in various mechanical systems, categorized into several types based on design and application. Deep groove ball bearings are versatile, handling radial and axial loads efficiently. Cylindrical roller bearings excel in high load capacities and minimal friction. Tapered roller bearings accommodate both radial and axial loads, making them ideal for automotive uses. Needle bearings, characterized by their slim design, provide high load support in limited spaces. Other variants include angular contact and thrust bearings, serving specific needs across industries.</p></p>

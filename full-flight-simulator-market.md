<p><p>The Full Flight Simulator market is categorized into three levels based on fidelity and regulatory compliance. Level A simulators offer basic visual systems and limited motion; they are suitable for procedural training. Level B simulators provide more advanced features, including enhanced visuals and motion systems, making them ideal for instrument training. Level C simulators offer full motion and advanced visual systems, enabling realistic flight experience and are used for type rating and extensive pilot training.</p></p>

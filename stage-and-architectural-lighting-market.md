<p><p>The stage and architectural lighting market consists of several key types: LED, Halogen, and Discharge lighting. LED lighting is energy-efficient, long-lasting, and offers versatile color options, making it popular for both stage performances and architectural displays. Halogen lights provide bright illumination with a warm color temperature but have shorter lifespans and higher energy consumption. Discharge lighting, including metal halide and high-pressure sodium lamps, is known for high lumen output, often used in larger venues for its powerful brightness and efficiency.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connector protection devices, specifically surge suppressors, are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their application and installation. Type I surge suppressors are designed for service entrance protection, often installed on the main power supply to safeguard against high-energy surges from external sources. In contrast, Type II surge suppressors are intended for point-of-use applications, providing additional protection at specific devices or outlets, thus enhancing overall system resilience. Both types are crucial for preventing damage to electronic equipment.</p></p>

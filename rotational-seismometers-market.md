<p><p>The rotational seismometers market is categorized primarily into single-axis and three-axis types. Single-axis seismometers measure rotation about one specific axis, making them suitable for applications requiring focused data collection in horizontal or vertical orientations. In contrast, three-axis seismometers capture rotational motion across all three spatial dimensions, providing a comprehensive view of seismic activity. This enables better analysis of ground motion and enhances the understanding of seismic events, making three-axis models particularly valuable in research and monitoring applications.</p></p>

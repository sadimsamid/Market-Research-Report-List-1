<p><p>The Anti-Nuclear Antibody (ANA) Test market encompasses various testing methodologies to detect autoantibodies associated with autoimmune diseases. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA) is widely used for its sensitivity and quantification of specific autoantibodies. Indirect immunofluorescence is a traditional method that visualizes antibodies by staining cells, providing patterns indicative of specific autoimmune conditions. Multiplex testing allows simultaneous analysis of multiple antibodies, enhancing diagnostic efficiency and providing comprehensive data for clinicians to tailor appropriate treatment strategies.</p></p>

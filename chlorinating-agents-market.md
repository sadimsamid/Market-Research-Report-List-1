<p><p>The chlorinating agents market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic chlorinating agents. Organic chlorinating agents, such as chlorobenzene and dichloroethane, are primarily used in the production of pesticides, pharmaceuticals, and dyes, providing various functionalities due to their carbon-based structure.

<p><p>HIV-1 screening tests include ELISA, rapid tests, and home access dried blood spot testing. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is a laboratory-based test that detects antibodies in blood, ensuring high accuracy. Rapid tests provide quick results, often within minutes, using blood or oral fluid, enhancing accessibility. Home access dried blood spot testing allows individuals to collect and send samples from home for lab analysis, promoting privacy and convenience. Together, these options cater to diverse needs in HIV screening.</p></p>

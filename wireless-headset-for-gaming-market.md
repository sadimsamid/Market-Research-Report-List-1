<p><strong>The Wireless Headset for Gaming Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>M Wave</li><li>Decimeter Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless headset for gaming market is categorized into three main types based on their transmission technology: Infrared, M Wave, and Decimeter Wave. Infrared headsets use light signals, offering a line-of-sight connection ideal for short distances, but limited mobility. M Wave headsets operate on microwave frequencies, providing greater range and less interference, suitable for immersive gaming experiences. Decimeter Wave headsets utilize UHF signals, balancing range and audio quality, making them popular among gamers who prioritize sound fidelity and connectivity stability.</p></p>

<p><p>The Zirconium Rod market encompasses various types, including hot rolled, forging, and cold working. Hot rolled zirconium rods are produced through high-temperature processing, enhancing ductility and reducing hardness, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility. Forged zirconium rods are shaped through mechanical deformation, providing high strength and improved structural integrity. Cold working involves shaping zirconium at lower temperatures, resulting in enhanced surface finish and precision. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, particularly in nuclear, chemical, and aerospace sectors.</p></p>

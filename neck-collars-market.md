<p><p>The neck collars market comprises two main types: soft neck collars and hard neck collars. Soft neck collars are made of flexible materials, providing comfort and support, often used for minor injuries or post-surgery recovery. They allow for some mobility while stabilizing the neck. In contrast, hard neck collars are rigid and provide maximum immobilization and support, typically used in cases of severe injuries, fractures, or during transportation of patients to prevent further damage. Each type serves specific medical needs effectively.</p></p>

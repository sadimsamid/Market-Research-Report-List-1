<p><p>Self-expanding coronary stents are medical devices designed to support arterial walls in patients with coronary artery disease. These stents are typically made from flexible materials that allow them to expand automatically upon deployment. This property helps in maintaining blood flow by preventing the re-narrowing of arteries, a common issue after angioplasty procedures. </p><p>The self-expanding coronary stents market is witnessing substantial growth, driven by technological advancements, increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, and rising geriatric populations. Additionally, the shift towards minimally invasive procedures is fueling demand, as these stents enhance patient comfort and recovery times. </p><p>Innovations, such as the development of drug-eluting stents that release medication to prevent thrombosis, are also influencing market dynamics. Moreover, strategic collaborations between key players and investments in research and development are expected to further bolster market expansion. </p><p>The Self-expanding Coronary Stents Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period, indicating strong market potential.

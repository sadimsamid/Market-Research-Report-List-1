<p><p>Connected Remote Healthcare Monitoring (RHM) encompasses two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise RHM involves the installation and management of monitoring systems directly within healthcare facilities, providing control over data and operations but requiring significant infrastructure investment. Conversely, cloud-based RHM leverages the internet for data storage and access, offering scalability, flexibility, and lower initial costs. This model enhances collaboration among healthcare providers and patients, facilitating real-time monitoring and timely interventions from virtually anywhere.</p></p>

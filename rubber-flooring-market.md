<p><p>The rubber flooring market is primarily divided into two types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber flooring is derived from latex harvested from rubber trees, offering excellent durability, elasticity, and eco-friendliness, making it suitable for residential and commercial spaces. In contrast, synthetic rubber flooring is produced from man-made materials, providing a wider range of colors and patterns, enhanced resistance to chemicals, and lower maintenance costs. Both types cater to diverse aesthetic and functional needs in various environments.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Cheese Starter Culture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mesophilic Type</li><li>Thermophilic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cheese starter culture market is categorized into two main types: mesophilic and thermophilic. Mesophilic cultures thrive at temperatures between 20-30°C, producing cheeses like cheddar and mozzarella with a smooth texture and mild flavor. In contrast, thermophilic cultures operate at higher temperatures, around 40-55°C, and are essential for cheeses such as parmesan and Greek yogurt, contributing to firmer textures and robust flavors. Each type plays a crucial role in cheese production, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>

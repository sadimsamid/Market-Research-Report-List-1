<p><strong>The Cloud Based Video Conferencing Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SVC Architecture</li><li>AVC Architecture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud-based video conferencing software is categorized into two main types: SVC (Scalable Video Coding) architecture and AVC (Advanced Video Coding) architecture. </p><p>SVC architecture allows for multiple resolutions of video to be transmitted simultaneously, enabling dynamic scalability and adaptation to varying bandwidth conditions, resulting in improved user experience. </p><p>In contrast, AVC architecture focuses on high-quality visual content with efficient compression, making it ideal for environments with stable bandwidth. Each architecture serves distinct needs, catering to different user requirements in video communication.</p></p>

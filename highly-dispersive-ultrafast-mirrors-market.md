Circular mirrors, on the other hand, provide uniform reflectance and are preferred for applications necessitating rotational symmetry. Both types are designed to enhance pulse duration and maintain beam quality in ultrafast applications, catering to industries such as telecommunications, medical imaging, and scientific research.</p></p>

<p><p>The pharmaceutical oral glass bottles market consists primarily of two types: low borosilicate glass and soda lime glass. Low borosilicate glass offers greater chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for storing sensitive pharmaceutical compounds. In contrast, soda lime glass is more affordable and widely used for non-critical applications, though it has lower resistance to chemical interactions and temperature fluctuations. Both types cater to different segments of the market, balancing cost-effectiveness and product protection needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The Intelligent Sprinkler Irrigation System market features two main types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers adjust irrigation schedules based on real-time weather data, optimizing water use by considering precipitation and evaporation rates. In contrast, sensor-based controllers utilize soil moisture and environmental sensors to determine the exact water needs of plants, delivering water precisely when required. Both types aim to enhance water efficiency, reduce waste, and promote sustainable agricultural practices while ensuring optimal plant health.</p></p>

<p><p>Silicone molding compounds (SMCs) encompass various manufacturing techniques, primarily press molding and injection molding. Press molding involves placing silicone material into a heated mold, applying pressure to shape it, ideal for larger, simpler parts. In contrast, injection molding forces heated silicone into a mold cavity under high pressure, facilitating intricate designs and efficient production of complex shapes. Both methods are crucial in various industries, including automotive and electronics, due to their ability to create durable and versatile silicone products.</p></p>

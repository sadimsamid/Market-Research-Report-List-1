<p><ul><li>Herbicide-tolerance (HT)</li><li>Insect-resistance (IR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional seeds market is segmented into two main types: herbicide-tolerance (HT) and insect-resistance (IR) seeds. HT seeds are engineered to withstand specific herbicides, allowing farmers to control weeds without damaging the crop. IR seeds are designed to resist insect pests, reducing the need for chemical pesticides and minimizing crop damage. Both types enhance crop yields and promote sustainability, addressing farmers' challenges while supporting effective pest and weed management strategies in agriculture.</p></p>

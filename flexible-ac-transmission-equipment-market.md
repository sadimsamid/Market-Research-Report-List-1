<p><strong>The Flexible AC Transmission Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Compensation</li><li>Series Compensation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Equipment (FACTS) market encompasses various technologies that enhance the efficiency and reliability of power transmission. Shunt compensation involves the addition of capacitors or reactors to the transmission line, improving voltage stability and reducing losses. In contrast, series compensation utilizes capacitors or inductors placed in-series with the transmission line, enhancing power flow and mitigating voltage drop. Both types contribute to better grid management and facilitate the integration of renewable energy sources into the electricity network.</p></p>

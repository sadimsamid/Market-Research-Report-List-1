<p><p>The pad printing inks market is primarily categorized into solvent-based and UV curing inks. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents for their formulation, offering good adhesion and durability on various substrates, making them ideal for diverse applications. In contrast, UV curing inks are designed to cure instantly when exposed to ultraviolet light, resulting in fast-drying and environmentally friendly prints. Both types cater to different needs in industries such as packaging, promotional items, and manufacturing, each with unique printing properties and advantages.</p></p>

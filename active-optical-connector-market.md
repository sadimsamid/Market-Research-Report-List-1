CFP (C Form-factor Pluggable) is utilized for high-speed applications, while SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) is common in smaller networks for flexible connections. Other connectors encompass specialized solutions to address niche requirements in optical connectivity. This diversity enhances network efficiency and capability across sectors.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Sintered NdFeB Magnet</li><li>Bonded NdFeB Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NdFeB magnets market is categorized into sintered and bonded types. Sintered NdFeB magnets are produced through a high-temperature process, resulting in high magnetic strength and stability, making them ideal for applications in motors and generators. Conversely, bonded NdFeB magnets combine magnetic powders with a polymer binder, allowing for more complex shapes and sizes with relatively lower magnetic strength. These are commonly used in consumer electronics and diverse industrial applications where flexibility in design is crucial.</p></p>

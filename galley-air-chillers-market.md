<p><p>The galley air chillers market consists of three main types: wall-mounted chillers, embedded chillers, and floor-standing chillers. Wall-mounted chillers are compact units installed on walls, saving floor space while providing efficient cooling. Embedded chillers are integrated into cabinetry or galley structures, offering a seamless appearance and maximizing available space. Floor-standing chillers are standalone units that provide substantial cooling capacity and flexibility in placement, making them ideal for larger galley environments. Each type caters to specific spatial and operational needs.</p></p>

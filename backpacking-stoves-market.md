<p><p>Backpacking stoves are categorized into three main fuel types: gas, liquid, and solid. Gas stoves utilize canisters filled with propane or butane, offering convenience and quick setup. Liquid stoves require fuel like white gas, providing versatility and efficient performance in varying temperatures. Solid fuel stoves burn tablets or discs, which are lightweight and easy to carry, though typically less efficient. Each type serves different needs, balancing factors such as weight, cooking speed, fuel availability, and environmental conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>The scroll saw market can be segmented into two primary types: C-arm and parallel arm saws. C-arm scroll saws feature a curved arm design, providing superior blade visibility and ease of maneuverability for intricate cuts. In contrast, parallel arm scroll saws employ a straight arm mechanism, delivering consistent tension and stability for precise, straight cuts. Both types cater to diverse user needs, with C-arm models favored for detailed work and parallel arm models preferred for accuracy and straightforward tasks.</p></p>

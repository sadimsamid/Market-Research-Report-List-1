<p><p>The semiconductor package substrates market encompasses various types designed for different applications. MCP (Multi-Chip Package) and UTCSP (Ultra-Thin Chip Scale Package) enable space-efficient designs. FC-CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package) provides excellent thermal and electrical performance. SiP (System in Package) integrates multiple components for compact solutions. PBGA (Plastic Ball Grid Array) and CSP (Chip Scale Package) cater to diverse package demands. BOC (Blade-On-Chip) and FMC (Fine Pitch Ball Grid Array) are emerging technologies, while automotive substrates focus on reliability and performance in vehicles.</p></p>

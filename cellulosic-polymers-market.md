<p><p>The **Cellulosic Polymers Market** includes **Cellulose Esters**, **Cellulose Ethers**, and **Regenerated Cellulose**. **Cellulose Esters** are derived by esterifying cellulose, offering properties like solubility and film-forming, used in coatings, films, and plastics. **Cellulose Ethers** are modified cellulose with enhanced water retention and thickening, widely used in construction, pharmaceuticals, and food. **Regenerated Cellulose** is chemically processed to create fibers like viscose and lyocell, used in textiles and packaging. Each type serves distinct industries, driven by sustainability and functional versatility.</p></p>

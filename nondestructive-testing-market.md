<p><strong>The Nondestructive Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eddy Current Inspection</li><li>Magnetic Particle</li><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Liquid Penetrant</li><li>Radiography</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nondestructive Testing (NDT) market includes various methods for evaluating materials without causing damage. Eddy Current Inspection uses electromagnetic induction to detect flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials using magnetic fields and iron particles. Electromagnetic testing assesses material properties via electromagnetic fields. Liquid Penetrant Testing reveals surface-breaking defects using dyes. Radiography utilizes X-rays or gamma rays for internal inspection, while Ultrasonic Testing employs high-frequency sound waves to detect discontinuities within materials.</p></p>

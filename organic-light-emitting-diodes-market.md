<p><ul><li>Hole Transport Layer Materials (HTL)</li><li>Electron Transport Layer Materials (ETL)</li><li>Electrode materials</li><li>Emissive Layer Materials (EML)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLED) market consists of several key components. Hole Transport Layer (HTL) materials facilitate the movement of positive charge carriers, enhancing device efficiency. Electron Transport Layer (ETL) materials assist in transporting negative charge carriers, ensuring balanced charge injection. Electrode materials, crucial for charge injection and extraction, influence the device's overall performance. Emissive Layer Materials (EML) are responsible for light emission and color properties, driving advancements in display technology, lighting solutions, and flexible electronics.</p></p>

The 20CrNiMo alloy is known for good toughness and weldability, while 30CrNiMo offers enhanced strength and wear resistance. The 40CrNiMo grade provides superior strength and fatigue resistance, making it ideal for high-stress environments, including aerospace and automotive applications.</p></p>

<p><p>Sputtering equipment cathodes are essential for thin-film deposition in various industries, including semiconductors and optical coatings. The market is divided into linear and circular types. Linear cathodes offer a longer target life and uniform deposition over large areas, making them ideal for wide films. In contrast, circular cathodes provide higher uniformity on smaller substrates and are often used for coatings requiring precise thickness. Each type caters to specific application needs, influencing their adoption across different sectors.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Railway Traction Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Type Traction Transformer</li><li>Shell Type Traction Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway traction transformer market is divided into two main types: core type and shell type traction transformers. Core type transformers feature a central core around which windings are wrapped, providing high efficiency and compact design suitable for various rail applications. In contrast, shell type transformers encapsulate the core within the windings, offering enhanced protection and durability. Both types serve to convert electrical energy for train propulsion, with specific advantages tailored to different operational environments and requirements in the railway sector.</p></p>

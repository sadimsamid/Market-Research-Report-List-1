<p><p>The food spray drying equipment market includes various types tailored for different applications. Rotary atomizer spray dryers utilize a rotating disc to create fine droplets, ideal for heat-sensitive materials. Nozzle atomizer spray dryers use high-pressure nozzles to produce droplets, suitable for diverse liquid feeds. Fluidized spray dryers maintain a fluidized bed for uniform particle drying. Closed-loop spray dryers recycle air for energy efficiency. Centrifugal spray dryers use centrifugal forces for atomization, while other types encompass specialized designs for unique processing needs.</p></p>

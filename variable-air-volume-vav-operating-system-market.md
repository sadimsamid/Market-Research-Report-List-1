<p><p>The Variable Air Volume (VAV) market includes several systems tailored for efficient air distribution. Single-duct VAV systems utilize a single duct to control airflow based on occupancy needs. Dual-duct VAV systems combine two air streams to regulate temperature and manage comfort. Induction VAV systems utilize induction principles to enhance air mixing and distribution. Fan-powered VAV systems include an auxiliary fan to boost airflow, ensuring consistent temperature and comfort throughout spaces. Each type offers unique benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
